## Differentiation

#### Definitions
Take a function \\(f: (a,b) \to \mathbb{R}\\) and \\(c \in (a,b)\\).
* If \\(\lim_{x \to c} \frac{f(x)-f(c)}{x-c}\\) exists, then \\(f\\) is _differentiable_ at \\(c\\) and we define the _derivative at \\(c\\)_ to ve the limit, denoted \\(f'(c)\\).
