## Cyclic Groups

#### Definition
A group $G$ is cyclic if it can be generated by a single element.

#### Theorem
If $m$ and $n$ are coprime integers, then $Z_{m} \times Z_{n} \cong Z_{mn}$.

#### Proof
This is a restatement of the chinese remainder theorem.
